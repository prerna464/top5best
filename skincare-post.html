Top 5 Best Vitamin C Serums Under ₹500 for Glowing Skin

By Top 5 Best Editorial · May 2026 · 5 min read

Vitamin C serums ranked by effectiveness, price, and real user results — India 2026.

We are constantly exposed to pollution and UV rays that damage and age our skin. Vitamin C serum works like an invisible layer of protection for your skin. It helps fight environmental stress that causes premature wrinkles, dark spots, and uneven skin tone.

Vitamin C serums are also very useful for reducing sunspots and acne scars. With regular use, these marks slowly begin to fade, and your skin starts looking brighter, smoother, and healthier.

✨ Personal Tips Before You Buy

  • Don't go out in the sun immediately after applying Vitamin C serum. Let the serum absorb properly, then apply sunscreen after 5–6 minutes. Wait around 30 minutes before stepping out.
  • If you are trying Vitamin C for the first time, use it at night after washing your face. You may notice fresher and brighter skin the next morning.
  • Choose a Vitamin C serum with niacinamide if you have open pores or oily skin. It helps improve skin texture and controls excess oil.
